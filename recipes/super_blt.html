Super BLT

Description

This is a new twist on an old favorite. Serve with chips or fresh fruit.

Ingredient list:
  • 8 slices bacon
  • 8 slices bread, toasted
  • ¼ cup guacamole
  • ¼ cup cream cheese
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 slices tomato
Steps:
  1. Place the bacon in a large, deep skillet, and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes. Drain the bacon slices on a paper towel-lined plate.
  2. PSpread the guacamole on 4 slices of toasted bread and the cream cheese on the remaining 4 slices. Arrange a lettuce leaf, tomato slice, and two pieces of bacon on top of 4 slices of bread and top with the remaining slices.
Enjoy!
